Financial and Reveue Management, and Taxation System

The FRMT system combines financial, revenue, taxation, monitoring, and verification modules to create a unified approach for council management. Designed with user-friendly features and sustainable strategies, this integrated system follows international standards and applicable laws. Its goal is to introduce innovations and advancements that contribute to socio-economic development in the regions it serves.

Revenue Management

Automate collections for businesses, properties, and market vendors. Real-time syncing for accurate financial tracking.

Financial Oversight
